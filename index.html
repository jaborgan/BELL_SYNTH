Final Presentation Akebono Integrated ADSR Visualizer Bell Synth

Use the keyboard and sliders to explore the Akebono bell synth.
by Gabriel Antonio Hernandez-Zepeda
• Instructions: Numbers (1 through 7) change the starting octave from a low fundamental pitch (27 Hertz) to very high frequency 'bell sounds' .
• Keys: 'a', 's', 'f', 'g', 'h', 'k', and 'l'. Corresponding to pentatonic notes.
• Circles of ADSR filter can be dragged to be manipulated
